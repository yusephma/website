mango cheesecake.

Ingredients

  • 90g Biscuits (Scotch fingers)
  • 55mL Milk
  • 200g Cream Cheese
  • 40g Sugar
  • 7g Gelatin
  • 1 Lemon
  • 200mL Thickened Cream
  • 100g Mango Puree
  • 30mL Water

Method

    Cake Base:

  1. Crush biscuits in a bag + 25mL milk → Mix
  2. Squish into a cake tin + Refrigerate

    3. Cake Mix:

  3. 200g cream cheese (softened) + 30g sugar → Mix
  4. 30mL milk + 5g gelatin → Heat + Add
  5. Add lemon zest + 1/3 lemon's juice → Mix
  6. Beat 200mL thickened cream + 20g sugar → Soft peaks → Add to main mixture + Fold
  7. Save 2-3 scoops of mixture and refrigerate
  8. Add 60g mango puree → Fold into main mixture
  9. Pour into cake tin → Tap + Smooth out → Freezer 20min
  10. Add saved mixture → Smooth out → Freezer 20min

    11. Mango Jelly:

  11. 30mL water + 2g gelatin → Heat up → 10g sugar + 40g mango puree → Mix and cool to room temp
  12. Add to cake mix → Refrigerate

strawberry shortcake.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 75g Sugar
  • 10mL Milk
  • 10g Unsalted Butter
  • 10g Honey
  • 55g Flour
  • 600mL Thickened Cream
  • 45g Condensed Milk
  • 1 Punnet of Strawberries
  • 40mL Water

Method

    Cake Base:

  1. 2 eggs → beat
  2. Add 55g sugar over water bath until warm and little bit foamy
  3. Melt 10g unsalted butter + 10mL milk → Hold, add later
  4. Beat until looks like shiny paint + 10g honey
  5. Add 55g flour → Fold
  6. Add milk & butter → Fold
  7. Bake 170℃ for 25-30min

    8. Syrup for Sponge:

  8. Dissolve 20g sugar + 40mL hot water

    9. Whipped Cream:

  9. 600mL thickened cream + 45g condensed milk
  10. Beat over ice bath until soft peaks → Refrigerate until use

tiramisu spongecake.

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 100g Sugar
  • 50g Flour
  • 10g Cocoa Powder
  • 30mL Milk
  • 5mL Oil (try without)
  • 200g Mascarpone Cheese
  • 100mL Thickened Cream
  • 3g Gelatin
  • 5mL Water
  • Instant Coffee

Method

    Sponge:

  1. 3 egg whites → Beat + 50g sugar + 2 egg yolks
  2. Add 50g flour + 10g cocoa powder → mix
  3. Add 30mL milk + 5mL oil → Mix
  4. Bake 170℃ for 30min

    5. Cream:

  5. 200g mascarpone cheese + 20g sugar + 2 egg yolks
  6. 1 egg white → beat + 30g sugar → Mix
  7. 100mL thickened cream → Beat + Mix
  8. Add 3g gelatin + 5mL warm water

    9. Coffee for Sponge:

  9. 2 spoons instant coffee + some hot water
